BRIGHT ACADEMY TSS Services

Multimedia

Our Multimedia program offers students the opportunity to explore various forms of digital media including graphics, animation, and video production. Students learn industry-standard software and techniques to express their creativity.

Software Development

In our Software development program, students learn programming languages, software design principles, and project management skills. They work on real-world projects to develop applications and software solutions.

Masonry

The masonry program provides hands-on training in construction techniques, materials, and safety practices. Students learn to build structures using bricks, stones, and concrete, preparing them for careers in the construction industry.

Automobile

Our automobile program covers vehicle maintenance, repair, and diagnostics. Students gain practical skills in automotive technology, including engine systems, electrical systems, and automotive engineering.
